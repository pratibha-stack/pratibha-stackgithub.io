About Solapur

Solapur is a historically and culturally rich district in Maharashtra. It is known for religious tourism, historic monuments and traditional textile industries.

The city was earlier known as Sonalpur and Sandalpur and today it is one of the largest districts in Maharashtra.

Languages such as Marathi, Kannada and Telugu are widely spoken here.

Solapur is famous for temples, handloom products, wildlife sanctuaries and vibrant local culture.
