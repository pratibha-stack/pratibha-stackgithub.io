Famous Food of Solapur

Solapuri Misal Pav

A spicy dish made from lentils topped with farsan and served with pav.

Bhakri and Thecha

Traditional millet bread served with spicy green chilli chutney.

Bhadang

A spicy puffed rice snack popular as street food.

Tambda Rassa

A fiery red curry commonly served with bhakri.

Poha and Vada Pav

Popular breakfast and street food items loved by locals and tourists.
