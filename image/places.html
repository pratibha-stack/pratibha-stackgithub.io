Pandharpur

Pandharpur is one of the most important pilgrimage sites in Maharashtra. It is famous for the Vitthal Rukmini Temple and the annual Wari pilgrimage which attracts lakhs of devotees.

Siddheshwar Temple

Siddheshwar Temple is located in the heart of Solapur city. The temple is dedicated to Lord Shiva and is surrounded by a beautiful lake which enhances its scenic beauty.

Tuljapur Temple

Tuljapur is famous for the Tulja Bhavani Temple which is one of the 51 Shakti Peethas of India. Devotees from across the country visit this temple throughout the year.
